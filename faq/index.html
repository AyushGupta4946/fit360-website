General

What is FitNambi?

FitNambi is a comprehensive platform designed to promote sports and fitness by offering tournament organization, fitness tracking, personalized fitness plans, and community engagement features.

How can I download the FitNambi app?

The FitNambi app is available for download on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Simply search for "FitNambi" and click the download button.

Account and Profile

How do I create an account on FitNambi?

To create an account, download the app, open it, and click on the "Sign Up" button. Fill in the required information, and follow the prompts to complete your registration.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information, go to the "My Profile" tab in the app, click on the "Edit Profile" button, and make the necessary changes. Don't forget to save your updates.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login screen. Enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

Tournament Participation

How do I register for a tournament?

To register for a tournament, go to the "tournaments" list screen, select the tournament you're interested in, and click the "Book Now" button. Follow the prompts to complete your registration.

Can I see the schedule of a tournament before registering?

Yes, you can view the tournament schedule by going to the tournament details after clicking the desired tournament from the tournament list screen. The schedule, along with other important information, will be displayed.

How will I be notified of my match timings?

You will receive notifications through the FitNambi app regarding your match timings. Please ensure notifications are enabled for the app in your phone settings.

What are the hygiene protocols for the tournaments?

Participants are expected to maintain personal hygiene, use hand sanitizers provided at the venue, and follow any additional guidelines provided by the tournament officials. Masks should be worn if required by current health guidelines.

Is there a registration fee for tournaments?

Yes, there is a registration fee for participating in tournaments. The fee varies by tournament and will be displayed during the registration process.

How do I pay the registration fee for a tournament?

After selecting the tournament and clicking "Book Now," you will be prompted to pay the registration fee via the FitNambi app. You can use various payment methods such as credit/debit cards, UPI, or mobile wallets using the Razor payment gateway. FitNambi app does not store any of your payment-related information.

Will I receive a confirmation after registering and paying the fee?

Yes, once your registration and payment are successful, you will receive a confirmation email and an in-app notification with your registration details.

Can I cancel my registration and get a refund?

At present, FitNambi does not support any general refund policy. Please contact your tournament organizer to discuss your specific case.

How can I contact FitNambi support?

For any issues or inquiries, you can contact FitNambi support through the "Help & Support" section in the app or by emailing info@fitnambi.com.

Where can I provide feedback about the app?

We welcome your feedback! You can provide feedback through the "Feedback" option in the tournament details page or by emailing info@fitnambi.com.

By providing clear and comprehensive FAQs within the FitNambi app, users will have easy access to the information they need, enhancing their overall experience and engagement with the platform.
